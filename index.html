Problem and Our Solution

The Problem

We spoke with 100+ EV drivers from throughout India and discovered a common complaint: India's EV charging system is broken. EV drivers have to go to the trouble of checking various applications to locate the most convenient charging stations. 8 out of 10 EV drivers said they had more than four different charging applications, which required them to register separate accounts and enter payment information for each of the accounts. With the broken EV charging system in the india, 70% of EV drivers own a second fuel vehicle for longer-range trips. Worse, two out of every ten EV owners leave their vehicle due to charging challenges.

Our Solution

Our mobile apps enable location, activation, and payment with only two clicks, thanks to an open charging protocol, complicated unique data flow, and cutting-edge user experience. Moreover, our application lets charger owners, such as local businesses, restaurants, and retail stores, to advertise their brands in order to attract EV customers and generate more business.

Why Now

The EV charging industry is rapidly expanding and poised to skyrocket. Following the surge in electric cars in India, the compound annual growth rate (CAGR) is predicted to hover around 46.6% between 2022 and 2030. Furthermore, governments' primary emphasis has shifted to combatting the climate problem, resulting a variety of new EV-friendly polices and mandates. Moreover, the EV charging industry is fragmented, with industry players competing for market share. By becoming the universal payment passport or "Visa in the EV charging industry," our solution has a promising future.

Overview

Hindustan Chargers is developing universal mobile applications and a website to let electric vehicle owners locate, activate, and pay for EV chargers from various EV charging station operators. Our solution to an inevitable problem in the EV space is consolidation. Hindustan Chargers' universal platform integrates all EV charging solutions into an unified platform.

Market Opportunity

Our total addressable market in the india is $324 million in 2022 and will grow to $2.1 billion in 2025. This is based on a bottoms-up model

Final Words

Our goal is to expand EV adoption in India, and we believe that by improving the charging experience for drivers, we will be able to encourage more people to switch to electric vehicles. Join us and be a part of this amazing initiative!
